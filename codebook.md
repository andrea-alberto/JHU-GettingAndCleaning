"activity" 
	The type of activity performed
	Possible values are:
		WALKING
	    WALKING_UPSTAIRS
	    WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
	    SITTING
	    STANDING
	    LAYING
	
"subject" 
	An identifier for the subject who performed the activity
